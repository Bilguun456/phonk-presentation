Section 01

What Is Phonk?

Phonk is a dark, bass-heavy subgenre of hip-hop and electronic music that fuses 1990s Memphis underground rap with modern trap production. Known for its lo-fi textures, heavy 808 bass, and haunting samples — it is the sound of the underground.

The Sound

Heavy 808 bass, chopped Memphis rap vocals, cowbells, vinyl crackle, dark synth pads — layered into a slow, hypnotic, menacing groove.

The Culture

Started as a tiny SoundCloud scene. Exploded globally via TikTok. Now the anthem of drift car culture, gaming, and underground youth worldwide.

The Mood

Dark, nocturnal, aggressive. Phonk doesn't ask for attention politely — it grabs you. Music for late nights, empty roads, and pushing limits.

The Name

A play on "funk" — that intangible quality of groove and feeling. Filtered through the dark, lo-fi lens of Memphis rap. That's the "phonk."

Section 02

History & Origin

1990s — Memphis, Tennessee

The Cassette Tape Era. Artists like Three 6 Mafia, DJ Paul & Juicy J built a raw underground rap scene — hand-dubbed tapes sold from car trunks. Horror samples, slow bass, lo-fi production. Never on radio. Pure underground.

Late 1990s — Houston, Texas

Chopped & Screwed. DJ Screw invents the technique of slowing down and chopping vocal samples — pitch-shifting, stuttering loops, syrupy tempo. This method becomes foundational to all future Phonk production.

2010–2016 — SoundCloud Underground

The Genre Is Born. DJ Smokey and Soudiere begin uploading Memphis-influenced productions to SoundCloud. The term "Phonk" emerges. A small but passionate community forms around the sound.

2018–2020 — Scene Expands

Global Producers Join. KORDHELL and a wave of Eastern European artists take Phonk in a harder, more aggressive direction. YouTube algorithms start spreading it to new audiences worldwide.

2021–2022 — TikTok Explosion

Drift Phonk Goes Viral. "Murder In My Mind" (KORDHELL) and "Metamorphosis" (Interworld) reach hundreds of millions of streams. Phonk becomes the global soundtrack of drift culture and gaming.

Section 03

Sound & Characteristics

Instruments & Equipment

  • Roland TR-808 — sub-frequency bass drum, the heartbeat of Phonk
  • Cowbells — metallic, rhythmic; instantly signals Memphis roots
  • Vinyl Samples — jazz, soul, funk records — pitched down, crackle preserved
  • Memphis Vocal Chops — fragments of 90s rap tapes used as melody
  • Dark Synth Pads — eerie, reverb-soaked, minor key atmosphere
  • FL Studio / Ableton — DAW of choice for most Phonk producers

Style & Techniques

  • 130–145 BPM — slow enough to feel heavy and hypnotic
  • Chopped & Screwed — vocals pitch-shifted down, cut into looping stutters
  • Sample Flipping — old records transformed into new melodies
  • Lo-Fi Aesthetic — tape hiss and vinyl crackle are features, not flaws
  • Heavy Distortion — everything pushed to the edge of breaking up
  • Dark / Minor Key — narrow emotional palette: menace, tension, power
🚗 Drift Phonk

Fast, distorted horns, aggressive — TikTok dominant

🌑 Dark Phonk

Slow, atmospheric, horror-influenced — purist form

🇧🇷 Brazilian Phonk

Fused with baile funk — Latin America's variant

⚡ Aggressive Phonk

Industrial-influenced, maximum distortion

Section 04

Key Artists

DJ SMOKEY
FOUNDING FATHER

Pioneer of modern Phonk on SoundCloud (2011–). Set the standard for Memphis-based sampling and lo-fi production. "Drippin In Phonk" defined the genre's blueprint.

SOUDIERE
UNDERGROUND ARCHITECT

Master of Memphis sample selection. "Hood Baby" is considered one of the most essential Phonk tracks ever — raw, authentic, hypnotic.

KORDHELL
MAINSTREAM BREAKTHROUGH

"Murder In My Mind" brought Phonk to hundreds of millions of listeners. Defined Drift Phonk and proved the genre could compete commercially.

GHOSTEMANE
CROSSOVER FORCE

Bridges Phonk, trap metal, and industrial. Brought underground dark aesthetics to alternative audiences. "Mercury" is a genre-crossing landmark.

INTERWORLD
ETHEREAL PHONK

"Metamorphosis" showed Phonk can be cinematic and emotional — soaring synths over dark Phonk rhythms. Massive global viral impact.

FLOYYMENOR
LATIN PHONK

Created the Latin Phonk movement — blending Phonk with reggaeton and Latin rhythms. Proved Phonk transcends language and geography.

MC RIDE
DEATH GRIPS INFLUENCE

Death Grips' confrontational, industrial hip-hop deeply influenced Phonk's aggressive wing — its "no compromise" ethos and maximalist darkness.

MONTAGEM AMOSTRA
BRAZILIAN PHONK

"Montagem" (Portuguese for "montage/edit") is a Brazilian funk style — rapid-fire sample cuts over heavy bass. This track blends baile funk energy with dark Phonk atmosphere. A perfect example of Brazilian Phonk: chaotic, bass-heavy, and completely addictive.

TIKI TIKI PHONK
TIKI TIKI PHONK
DRIFT / AGGRESSIVE PHONK

"Tiki Tiki" refers to the rapid 16th-note hi-hat pattern — the skittering mechanical rhythm that defines aggressive drift Phonk. This viral style took over TikTok and YouTube gaming edits. The relentless "tiki-tiki" energy makes it impossible to sit still.

ARIIS — BRAZILIAN FUNK PHONK

"Gozalo" means "enjoying it" in Brazilian slang. Ariis blends funk carioca's bouncy rhythm with dark Phonk production — heavy 808s, distorted synths, infectious groove. Represents how Brazilian producers gave Phonk a warmer, more danceable identity without losing its edge.
